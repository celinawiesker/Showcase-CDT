The Inspiration for the game

I was inspired to create this game by the fact that my grandma really enjoys playing tablet games and her happiness. We have played a matching game before but the design was not really appealing to my grandma. Over the last years we discovered that she likes colurful imagery and items. When we had a family party she really enjoyed playing with the games of my little cousins. I decided to take a look at toddler games and to make it as appealing for her as possible. Thus I tested some toddler games with her and also used those games as an inspiration. Below you can find some images that inspired me.
